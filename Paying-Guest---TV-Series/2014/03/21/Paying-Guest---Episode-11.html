Paying Guest is a TV Series about Krishnakant Trivedi (Subbiraj) with his wife, Sharda (Urmila Bhatt), who are staying in an old bungalow of suburban Bombay . In order to kill their time they plan to keep paying guest. So the guests arrive each with a different story.
