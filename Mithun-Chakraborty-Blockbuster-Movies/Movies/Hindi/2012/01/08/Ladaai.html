Ladaai

Jan 8th, 2012

After bring framed for a murder he did not commit, Dindayal (Mithun Chakraborty) is sentenced to prison for life. 18 years later, Dindayal is released and locates Judge Shakuntala(Rekha), the former advocate who sent him to jail. To prove his innocence, He kills himself in her house, but before dying asks Shakuntala to avenge his death by locating the real murderers. Shakuntala hides his death from everyone and sets out to find the murderer and also find Dindayal’s wife and son. She crosses paths with Shera (Mithun Chakraborty), who is look alike of Dindayal and recruits him to track down three possible suspects, Radheyshyam, Nissar Khan, and Peter. But things take a violent turn when Shera uncovers that the suspected trio are not the real murderers and that the real mastermind is a person who nobody will think of as a criminal. Who is the wolf in sheep’s clothing?Can Shera unmask the mastermind before its too late?
