Watch Bollywood Movie ‘Meri Pyari Bahania Banegi Dulhania’ (2001) Starring: Mithun Chakraborty, Raageshwari Sachdev. Directed by Jayant Gilatar Produced by Vijay Upadhyan Music by Madhu Burman Gopal Burman. Synopsis: Keshav (Mithun) A porter by profession lives with his sister Mehandi (Raageshwari) who he loves more than his life, and a drunkard father Rasiklal (Inderjit Sachdev)in a small village. The only moto in Keshav’s life is to get Mehandi married and settle her happily. At the same time he falls in love with Janki (Sheena) a weaver girl whose family also lives int he same village. Janki also loves him madly. At the same time there is Lajo (Sindhor) also a porter madly loves Keshav and wants to marry him.She keep a proposal to her mother that if her brother Birjoo (Vishwajeet Pradhan) marries Mehandi, Keshav in return will get married to Lajo. But destiny has some other thing in store for everybody. When Birjoo comes back from dobh refuses to marry Mehandi because shw is dumb. And there is a clash between Keshav and Birjoo.
