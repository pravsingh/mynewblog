Amne Samne - Mithun Chakraborty, Bindiya Goswami, Aarati Gupta - Bollywood Action Romantic Movie

Mar 22nd, 2014

Gopi (Mithun Chakraborty) is a honest rustic soul who comes to the city after a brutal clash with the village landlord. Once in the metropolis, fate intervenes and Gopi lands right into the middle of a glamorous lifestyle led by Johny. Johny, who happens to be a look alike of Gopi, is a popular singer who, unknown to the world, is also a crafty member of the underworld. Johny decides to use the naive Gopi in escaping the law. He along with his accomplice Rita, get Gopi to impersonate him. Gopi soon finds himself running not only from the police, the Central Bureau of Investigation, but also from gangsters owing allegiance to the mighty international don named Supremo – who believes that Gopi has a stolen goods worth millions. How will Gopi free himself from this deadly game of deception?
