Tyaagi - Rajnikanth, Mithun Chakraborty

Mar 22nd, 2014

Jagdish (Mithun Chakraborty) lives a simple and honest life with his wife Savitri. Jagdish’s co-workers and cruel parents prey on his innocence and take advantage of him. Jagdish is tormented by corruption and atrocities around him and he transforms himself into a man of absolute power. He starts moonlighting as a vigilante named Alfred to fight crime and serve justice. A man named Ranjit sets his eyes on Savitri and schemes devious plans to break her marriage. What price will Jagdish pay to vanquish evil as Alfred? Can he save his wife before it’s too late?
