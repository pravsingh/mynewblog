Ashanti

Mar 22nd, 2014

Ashanti is a Bollywood Action Movie, starring Parvenn Babi, Zeenat Aman, Shabana Azmi, Mithun Chakraborty, Rajesh Khanna. Honest and dedicated Police Inspector Kumar Chandra Singh intercepts a bank robbery. He is in the process of arresting the bank robbers. However, he himself becomes a suspect and is held with a suitcase containing some of the cash stolen from that bank.Kumar is arrested and sentenced to prison. Kumar’s mother could not bear this shock and passes away. When he is released from prison, he has only one goal in his life – to find the culprits responsible for the destruction and humiliation. Kamini, Sunita, Sonia and Shankar Dada support Kumar for the same. Will Kumar succeed in his mission?
