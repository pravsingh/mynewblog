Daag - the Burning Fire

May 21st, 2013

The Story is about Yadagiri who is a handicapped mafia leader in Hyderabad and KR is a rich business man. Bhagyamati is daughter of Yadagiri. Bobby is son of KR. Bobby meets Bhagyamati in a club and falls in love with her. They later find out that their fathers are arch rivals. Bhagyamati’s mother was killed in an accident involving their fathers. Knowing this Bobby and Bhagyamati elope. But due to unavoidable circumstances they come back and are injured in a bomb blast. Then their fathers apologize and make up and in the end bobby and Bhagyamati are married.
