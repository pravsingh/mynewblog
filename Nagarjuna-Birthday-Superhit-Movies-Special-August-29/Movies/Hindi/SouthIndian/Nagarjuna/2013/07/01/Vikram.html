Vikram

Jul 1st, 2013

The Story is about a garage mechanic, Vikram, is the Chief mechanic for the upcoming Roots Company Challenge 1991 Automotive rally race. which starts via the route from Chennai coast to Goa coast, a route which is noted. Retired Major Harischandra prasad is the Chief guest for the Rally, his daughter Padmini also competes for the Roots Challenge Trophy. Further, Vikram’s journalist friend Sudhakar (Chinna) after witnessing the murder of city D.I.G., gets murdered by the group of goons near his garrage, The D.I.G.’S murder happens to be planned by a high profile unknown criminal. Police commissioner who is a close associate to Harischandra prasad is up to unweaving the mysterious murder of D.I.G and the journalist. On the other hand, Vikram finds himself beaten by the goons after he comes to the assistance of his journalist friend. When he regains his senses he finds himself with a revolver in his hand and in Police custody, arrested and charged with the homicide of the very friend he tried to assist. With evidence pointing toward his guilt, he has virtually no defense and may well spend the rest of his life in prison or be hanged. He manages to escape from the prison with the help of Vikram’s garage assistant, Golconda through the journalist’s friend in a water tanker. Interestingly, in guise of the journalist’s friend (joseph),the high profile criminal’s henchmen is hidden in the tanker. The criminal’s henchmen plans to blast the water tanker and kill Vikram who flees from prison in the same water tanker. But, after a battle with the henchmen,inside the water tanker, Vikram escapes and jumps from the water tanker into a lake and the henchmen dies in the water tanker explosion. Police send Vikram’s case to CBI as they come to a conclusion that it was Vikram who was killed in the water tanker explosion. Now, Vikram gets to participate in the ‘one halt race’ by joining at a mile stone of the route, being in guise of the garage assistant’s race car . Vikram also finds out that among 3 cars of the rally, the photogarphs of D.I.G’s murder caught by his journalist friend are hidden. In another twist, Padmini happens to drive one of the 3 cars where in the photographs were hidden.
