Ek Hasina Thi

Jan 11th, 2013

Ek Hasina Thi is the latest Bollywood Indian psychological crime thriller film noir starring Urmila Matondkar Saif Ali Khan ,Seema Biswas, directed by Sriram Raghvan and Produced By Ram Gopal Verma. A woman (Urmila) falls for a charming and mysterious businessman(Saif Ali Khan). The whirlwind romance turns sour when she is framed for his underworld crimes. Now, finally out of prison she is ready for sweet revenge.
