Joggers Park

Jul 21st, 2010

Joggers Park – Full Length Classic Bollywood Movie – With English Subtitles – 2003. Joggers Park Bollywood film made in English and Hindi. It is directed by Anant Balani and written by Subhash Ghai. It stars Bengali actor Victor Bannerjee, Perizaad Zorabian and Divya Dutta in lead roles. It was filmed on location in Mumbai’s Jogger’s Park.Music composed by Tabun Sutradhar, Lyricist by Zameer Kazmi, Sameer, Usha Uthup, Subhash Ghai, Singers – Asha Bhonsle, Jagjit Singh, Usha Uthup,Adnan Sami, Sudesh Bhonsle, Producer by Subhash Ghai & Prem Pillai. Cast: Victor Banerjee, Parizaad Zorabian, Divya Dutta, Anant Mahadevan, Khalid Siddiqui, Abha Dhulia, Hiten Tejwani, Dinyar Contractor, Vivek Vasvani, Manoj Joshi, Sushil Parashar.
