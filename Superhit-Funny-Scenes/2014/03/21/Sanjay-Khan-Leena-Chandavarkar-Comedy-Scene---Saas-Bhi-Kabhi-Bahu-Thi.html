Sanjay Khan Leena Chandavarkar Comedy Scene - Saas Bhi Kabhi Bahu Thi

Mar 21st, 2014

Check out the comedy scene from the classic bollywood family drama hindi movie Saas Bhi Kabhi Bahu Thi (1970) where Deepak (Sanjay Khan) spots an unattended baby crying in a pram and hence blames Sadhana (Leena Chandavarkar) who is sitting nearby assuming its her baby. Deepak mistakenly shouts at her for not paying enough attention to her child. Continue watching to see what happens to Deepak when he himself decides to take the baby for a stroll. The film stars Sanjay Khan, Leena Chandavarkar, Jagdeep, Lalita Pawar, Om Prakash & Jayshree T. Directed by V. Madhusudan Rao. Produced by Vasu Menon. Music by R.D Burman.
