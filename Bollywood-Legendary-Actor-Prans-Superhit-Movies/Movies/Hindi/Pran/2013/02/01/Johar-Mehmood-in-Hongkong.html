Johar Mehmood in Hongkong

Feb 1st, 2013

The Story reveals about two boys Ramesh and Mahesh who are tricksters of the highest level and they try to rob a rich girl Sonia by posing as a doctor and a patient. In order to win over each other in doing the task first, they go through various sequences involving the doctor torturing the patient in many hilarious ways. But a mysterious rich man goes one up against them and they find themselves looking for a girl named Sonia in Hong Kong. They realise that innocent Sonia is the key to atomic weapons that can be a threat to the country(India). so then they convince sonia and rob the atomic formula with help of sonia. After that the villans start chasing them in search of the formula.
