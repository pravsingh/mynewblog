Aaj Ka Shoorveer

May 21st, 2013

The Story is about Gemini who is an up and rising goon in Chintadripet who aspires to become a big dhada. Teja is another contemporary rowdy. When Gemini’s friend Jeeva is killed by Teja’s gang member, Gemini and his gang search for the murderer and kill him. This begins a rivalry between Gemini and Teja. One day a businessman approaches Gemini to evict people from a market in order to build a shopping complex. But since the market is in his control, Gemini refuses the offer. The businessman eventually approaches Teja to get the job done. Gemini sends his sidekick Kai to Teja’s gang in the premise of an altercation between them. Using his brilliant tactics, Gemini fools and wins against Teja which drives him crazy. Gemini meets a Marwari girl Manisha Natwarlal and falls in love with her. To pursue her, he joins an evening college as her classmate. Eventually she too falls for him. Meanwhile Annamma, the mother of the gang member killed by Gemini, approaches him and becomes his cook. She projects herself as an aging woman who has to work for getting her eye operated, all the while plotting to poison them one day. In the meantime, Singaperumal, an astute police officer is posted as the DGP. Keen on wiping out rowdyism, he arrests both Gemini and Teja. Understanding their rivalry, the DGP puts them in a private cell so they can beat each other to death and put an end to rowdyism. Instead, Gemini convinces Teja to plead guilty and request for a chance to turn over a new leaf. Gemini’s trick works and they are released. Since Gemini was actually arrested while at college, Manisha gets to know about him and starts ignoring him. To win his love back, Gemini reforms his ways. Now that Gemini and co. have regretted their actions, Annamma reveals her true identity and forgives them. Teja goes back to his gang and continues smuggling goods. He keeps on disturbing Gemini and wants him to help him out in his business. But instead Gemini informs about Teja and his activities to the DGP. Teja gets prosecuted and gets a prison term. A few months later, Singaperumal gets transferred to the transport department and is posted as the new DGP. A corrupt officer, the new DGP takes Teja’s side and releases him. Now Teja and the new DGP urge Gemini to help them in the business. But he still tries to stay away from them. In order to force Gemini back to his old business, Teja gets a member of Gemini’s gang, Isaac to conspire against them. With Isaac’s help, Teja plots and kills Kai. To avenge for the death, Gemini decides to kill Teja. During the ensuing fight, the new DGP arrives and fires at Gemini killing him. It is later revealed that Gemini had actually interchanged his dress with Teja’s and the DGP have killed Teja assuming it to be Gemini. Later the film ends with all problems being solved, and Gemini could again lead a fresh life with Manisha.
