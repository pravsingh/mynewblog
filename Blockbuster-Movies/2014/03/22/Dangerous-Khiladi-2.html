Dangerous Khiladi 2

Mar 22nd, 2014

The Story is about Corrupt Central minister, is facing accusations over possessing black money of one lakh crore, and laundering it into Europe, through his un-suspecting daughter, Akanksha, a psychology student who goes to Barcelona to pursue higher education. Akanksha finds a diary in her flat in Barcelona, and out of curiosity she starts reading the book which unfolds the romantic drama between Sanju Reddy and Komal Shankarabharanam. Akanksha gets engaged in Paris with her father’s hench man. Incidentally, she runs into Sanju Reddy, and gets to know him. Sanju is an engineer turned lead guitarist of a music band, and makes his living through street and stage performances. As narrated in the diary, Komal comes from an orthodox Telugu Brahmin family, into classical music, and ends up learning violin, at a musical school in Barcelona under Fiddle-professor Brahma. In a twist of fate, she falls in love with Sanju and gets approval for their inter caste marriage, from their parents in India. Komal unknowingly gets caught after a video footage that she accidentally shot while a henchman kills a Spanish Ambassador, in the process of money laundering. Sanju saves Komal from the henchman, on a couple of occasions; this is where the story in the diary ends abruptly. Out of curiosity Akanksha starts questioning Sanju about their love story and in the process ends up with disturbing facts. On continuous asking by Akanksha, Sanju tells her that Komal is dead. It is then shown in flashback that for fixing the marriage of Sanju & Komal, their parents had come to Spain. It was at that time that Komal is kidnapped by the brother of Shawar Ali & when Sanju comes to save her, Komal is murdered by the brother of Shawar Ali. In a fit of rage, Sanju bashes up the whole gang & the brother of Shawar Ali goes into coma. On hearing this, Akanksha feels pity for Sanju & falls in love with him. She also tries to bring Sanju out of the memory of Komal but in vain. Akanksha runs to Sanju to tell him about Komal and at that time, Sanju gets surrounded by the men of Shawar Ali. Sanju while bashing up his men, tells Akanksha that it was his plan to trap Akanksha in his love to avenge the death of his & Komal’s parents at the hands of Shawar Ali. It is then shown in flashback that after beating up the brother of Shawar Ali, Sanju had taken the body of Komal to the Hospital, from where Sanju’s Dad calls up the Central Minister to arrest Shawar Ali in Spain, after watching the video footage. The Minister then calls up Shawar Ali to finish off this trouble in order to save himself. Shawar Ali, then goes to the hospital & kills Sanju, his parents as well as Komal’s parents. However, Sanju & Komal do not die. It is then revealed that Sanju has been all this time waiting to get his revenge & has made Akanksha his pawn in this game & it was him only who faxed their photo in order to make Shawar Ali jealous, so that Shawar Ali will come himself to kill Sanju.
