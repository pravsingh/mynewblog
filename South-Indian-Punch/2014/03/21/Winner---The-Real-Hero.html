Winner - the Real Hero

Mar 21st, 2014

[–]

Sakthi (Prashanth), who often gets into fights, is packed off to his grandparents home in the village. There he meets Neelima (Kiran) in a marriage ceremony and falls in love with her. But Neelima’s marriage is already fixed with the village brute, Karan. Meanwhile, a dangerous goon named Virender is in pursuit of Neelima to exact revenge against her father. Sakthi sets out to fight Karan, Virender and Neelima’s powerful father to triumph in his love. Will Sakthi decimate his enemies and rise to supremacy as the true winner?
