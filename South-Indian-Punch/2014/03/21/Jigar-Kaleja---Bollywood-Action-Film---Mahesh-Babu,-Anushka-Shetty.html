Jigar Kaleja - Bollywood Action Film - Mahesh Babu, Anushka Shetty

Mar 21st, 2014

Watch action comedy movie ‘Jigar Kaleja’, the hindi dubbed version of the South Indian film ‘Khaleja’ (2010) directed by Trivikram Srinivas.

Synopsis: The remote village of Pali is plagued by a mystery illness that starts to kill the inhabitants one by one and the village priest predicts that their savior will soon come down to rescue them. Meanwhile, Raju (Mahesh Babu), a taxi driver happens to unwittingly get mixed up in a case of brutal murder of two people. Since his taxi is battered in the incident, he goes to Rajasthan to handover compensation to one of the victim’s family. On his way back he crosses paths with Subhashini (Anushka) and runs into trouble with GK’s (Prakash Raj) Goons. After escaping certain death, they both run into the village of Pali. Raju is hailed as a God by the villagers and they crown him as their savior. How are Raju, Subhashini, GK and the village of Pali interlinked? Can Raju save the village of Pali in time?
