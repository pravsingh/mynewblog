Bose’s is an honest young man with strong principles and wants to be a police officer. Under tragic circumstances he is forced to take the law in his hands and ends up hating the police. He prowls the streets taking out the local goons and politicians who destroy the peace of the society. But his life changes when he meets with IPS officer Arvind Kumar who guides him to become a police officer. After his training, Bose is posted in the city of Vizag which is controlled by wicked Politician Ratnam and his criminal gang head, Durga. A war escalates when Bose stands up against the tyrannical men and vows to put an end to them. Can Bose destroy the corrupt politicians and dangerous criminals with his brute force? How much blood will be shed until true justice is served?
