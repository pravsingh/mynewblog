Padosan

Mar 22nd, 2014

Bollywood classic – Padosan 1968, is the hilarious story of how destiny brings two opposites together. Bhola – Sunil Dutt is a straightforward and unlettered village bumpkin while Bindu – Saira Bano is a modern and educated rich girl who is independent of spirit and highly accomplished in the arts. While Bhola falls in love with Bindu, she ridicules him and only feels contempt for him. Her music teacher – Mehmood encourages her to heap more insults at Bhola.
