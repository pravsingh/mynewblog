Himmat

Mar 21st, 2014


Watch the Bollywood Action film Himmat (1996) starring Sunny Deol, Tabu, Shilpa Shetty, Naseeruddin Shah, Gulshan Grover, Mohan Joshi & Mukesh Khanna. Directed by Sunil Sharma & Produced by Sangeeta Singh. Music composed by Anand-Milind.

Synopsis: The story revolves around two Indian Secret Service Agents, Abdul & Ajay who live together as family. Abdul is assigned on Project M that deals with India’s nuclear capability for defense, whereas Ajay sets of to Switzerland for a vacation. Ajay chances a meeting with beautiful Anju & falls in love with her. Soon he returns to India only to learn that Abdul was killed while working on Project M. Later he is assigned to complete the project & find Abdul’s assassins. Issues evovles when he discovers that Anju’s father is one of the masterminds behind Abdul’s assassins. Will Ajay be able to solve the case?
