Mission the Last War - Bollywood Action Film

Mar 22nd, 2014

Maharaja (Amrish Puri), a notorious criminal who controls the red light areas, abducts a college girl named Chanda and forces her into prostitution. Chanda’s lover Krishna sets out to find her but his attempts are foiled by Maharaja. Krishna’s brother Biju, an Airforce commander vows to find Chanda and his brother but fails to get a lead on Maharaja’s location. He is joined by Succhi, a young man who’s friend meets the same fate as Chanda. Both men unite to track down Maharaja’s location and decimate his crime empire to rise supreme in the war against injustice.
